A la carte menu at Ego at The Grapes, Shrewsbury

Our a la carte menu at Ego at The Grapes, Shrewsbury, brings delicious,  freshly cooked food from across the Mediterranean including French, Spanish, Italian, Greek and Moroccan food. Join us and enjoy everything from chargrilled steaks, kebabs, fresh fish and seafood, pizza, pasta and paella, which can be enjoyed inside or outside in our beautiful garden space.
